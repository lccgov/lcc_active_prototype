Swim

What is it?

Swimming can be enjoyed in public swimming pools, leisure clubs, gyms or in open water. Open water swimming takes place outside in open bodies of water such as lakes, rivers, reservoirs and the sea.

Why is it good for me?

  • Swimming is a low impact exercise
  • Works almost every muscle in your body without putting your joints under stress
  • Excellent all-round activity

Is it for me?

Swimming is a sport for all ages and abilities. There is a type of swimming to suit everyone.
Open water swimming can require a higher level of strength and water confidence so it it best to start in an indoor pool and build up to outdoor swimming. There is an outdoor pool in Ilkley within easy travelling distance from Leeds.

Swimming is an easily accessible sport and there are classes all across Leeds for all abilities including women only lane swimming and swimming lessons. Grab your swimming costume and head to your local leisure centre or gym.

How much does it cost?

A swimming session at a public baths usually costs around £5. The cost of swimming lessons can vary depending on where you go. If you want one to one lessons then these will be more expensive than group lessons. If you want to join a swimming club then they usually have annual fees and additional coaching fees.

If you join a gym with a pool attached it is usually free to use the pool but you will have to pay an annual or monthly gym subscription.

What equipment do I need?

  • A swimming costume
  • Towel
  • You might also want to use swimming goggles, swimming hat and ear plugs

Become an ambassador

We want you to help us persuade more women and girls to get active, whatever their age, size, background or ability.

What is an Ambassador?

A Leeds Girls Can Ambassador: Goes through an appropriate recruitment process and signs up to Leeds City Council and Sport England’s policies and procedures. The main aim of an Ambassador is to get more women and girls in Leeds physically active. Ambassadors are required to attend monthly meetings which are vital for receiving guidance and insight around This Girl Can, this helps support their role. An Ambassador is not paid for their time, they are not required to lead a group and the roles can vary.
